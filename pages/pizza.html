Delicious Taco Pizza

Ingredients

Name Amount
pound ground beef 1
envelope taco seasoning 1
water 1 cup
prebaked 12-inch pizza crusts 2
can (16 ounces) refried beans 1
salsa 3/4 cup
coarsely crushed tortilla chips 2 cups
shredded cheddar cheese 2 cups
Optional: shredded lettuce and chopped tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a large skillet, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink; drain.
    Stir in taco seasoning and water. Bring to a boil; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes; set aside.

  2. Place crusts on ungreased pizza pans or baking sheets. Combine beans and salsa; spread over crusts.
    Top with beef mixture, chips and cheese.

  3. Bake until heated through and cheese is melted, 13-16 minutes. If desired, sprinkle with lettuce and tomatoes.
